FamiljeFärd Logotype - 2024

Timeline: 18 december 2023 - 15 januari 2024
My role(s): Graphic Designer & Video editor

During the first course module “Design Methods and Creative Processes” a collaboration with Visit Stockholm was carried out to explore creative solutions within the theme “travel without traveling”.

Different projects were created and this forms the basis for this work. The focus was on developing a travel planner for Stockholm, aimed at the family rather than being a strictly efficient travel planner for the parents.

The FamiljeFärd service was aimed at parents and children, especially those who wanted to engage their children in the planning. It enabled the children’s participation and responsibility in choosing a travel planner that suited their passions, age and budget.

FamiljeFärd strove to create a functional and entertaining travel planning service for parents and children travelling around Stockholm. The goal was that the children were actively included in the planning and looked forward to the whole trip. The goal was to create joy and adventure for both parents and children through an integrated family experience.

The ambition was to make the journey fun, exciting and inclusive for the whole family. FamiljeFärd’s visual identity was characterised by colourful and playful elements that reflected joy and creativity. The typeface, with geometric shapes, complemented the logo and the overall feeling of the service. It was easy to read and aimed not to overshadow the main purpose to find a guide that suited each family. The logotype consisted of three recurring colours for FamiljeFärd. It created contrast and conveyed a playful feeling while reflecting the essence of the service.
