Logotypes

Kvarteret Thai - 2024

Timeline: 12 november 2024 - 14 november 2024
My role(s): Graphic Designer

The logotype was created for the restaurant “Kvarteret Thai” with the aim of strengthening their connection to both Thailand and food.

To achieve this, the Thai flag was incorporated into the design, while the letter “H” was stylized with a fork to symbolize dining.

The slogan “Taste of Thailand” further emphasizes the restaurant’s identity and culinary focus.
