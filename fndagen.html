FN DAGEN - 2023

Timeline: 2023
My role(s): Graphic Designer

During my studies at Södertörn University, we created different posters for "FN DAGEN 2024". I was assigned “1. Ingen Fattigdom” (eng. 1. No Poverty, and to highlight this I chose to design the poster in a constructivist style and apply various symbols that represent or are associated with no poverty, combined with headlines that clearly describe the mission.
